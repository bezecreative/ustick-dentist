  • Staff that we feel is second to none — our patients agree
  • Most insurances accepted; in-house savings plans available
  • Family and locally owned, deeply rooted in the Boise community

Our Team

Meet the Doctors

Owner & Lead Dentist

Dr. Vince Rigby

Born in Salmon, Idaho and raised in Reno, Nevada, Dr. Rigby earned his bachelor's degree in Health Sciences from the University of Nevada, then his dental degree from the University of Colorado Health Sciences Center in 1999.

After five years at community health centers in Payette and Nyssa, he became owner of Ustick Dental Office. A member of the American Dental Association and the Southwest Idaho Dental Society, Dr. Rigby is also the organizer of Ustick Dental Cares Day — a free dentistry event offered annually to the community.

Associate Dentist

Dr. Gregory Booth

A Boise native and proud Gonzaga Bulldogs fan, Dr. Booth received his DDS from Creighton University in 2008 and completed a two-year Advanced Education in General Dentistry residency at the University of Missouri in Kansas City. He has been with Ustick Dental since 2010.

Dr. Booth practices a full range of general dentistry including implants, composite restorations, crowns and bridges, root canals, and dentures. His top priority is being genuinely present for his patients and delivering the care they deserve.

Our entire team is committed to delivering exceptional oral healthcare in a warm, welcoming environment. We look forward to helping you achieve a healthy, beautiful smile.
